Top Negative Review Topics

Subscription Fatigue

Users are tired of paying monthly for simple timers. A Lifetime Unlock or purely free model with cosmetic IAP is highly requested.

Battery Drain & Heat

Many apps keep the screen awake or run heavy animations. Efficient background processing is a must-have.

Lack of Ecosystem Sync

Users expect their timer to sync seamlessly between iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch. Standalone apps are being deleted.
